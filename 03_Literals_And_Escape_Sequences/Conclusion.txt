- What is literals?
  Literals are data used for representing fixed values. They can be used directly in the code.

- Can you assign different values to a literal?
  You can't assign different values to literals

- What are literal types?
  Decimal, Octal, Hexadecimal, Floats, Characters, Strings, Escape-Sequences

- Why do we need escape sequence literals?
  They are primarily used to put nonprintable characters in character and string literals.
