Literal Types:

1. Integer Literals:
   1. decimal(base 10)
   2. octal(base 8)
   3. hexadecimal(base 16)

   Decimal: 0, -9, 22 etc
   Octal: 021, 077, 033 etc
   Hexadecimal: 0x7f, 0x51A, 0x632 etc

In C++ programming, octal starts with a 0, and hexadecimal starts with 0x

2. Floating-Point Literals: 1.5, 13.7, 1.99, -8.81
3. Characters Literals: A, b, z, $, #
4. Escape Sequences Literals: \n, \t, \\, \a, \", \'
